## Section 1 — Sum assured and age alignment

For **term** products, the maximum sum assured must align with the published grid in the pricing system. Where the applicant age exceeds the product maximum entry age, the case must be **postponed** or offered an alternate product.

### Table A — Illustrative maximum sum assured (INR)

| Age band (years) | Maximum sum assured (INR) |
|------------------|---------------------------|
| 18–35            | 2,00,00,000               |

| 51–65            | 50,00,000                 |

These figures are **illustrative** for demo datasets; production limits are maintained in approved actuarial tables.

## Section 2 — Smoker status

For product line **Flexi Growth ULIP** (internal code **PRD-ULIP-003**), **current smokers** are not eligible under the simplified online journey. Such applications must be referred to manual underwriting or declined per channel rules.

## Section 3 — Medical and financial referrals

Decline reason **R-MED-01** applies when medical questionnaire responses indicate elevated risk that cannot be accepted on standard terms. Decline reason **R-FIN-02** applies when income or occupation class does not support the proposed sum assured.

## Section 4 — State-level operations

Applications sourced from **Maharashtra (MH)**, **Karnataka (KA)**, **Tamil Nadu (TN)**, **Delhi (DL)**, and **Gujarat (GJ)** are supported in this demo. Channel mix may vary by state; refer to distribution playbooks for details.
