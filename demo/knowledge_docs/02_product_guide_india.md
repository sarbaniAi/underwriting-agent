# Product Guide — Synthetic India Life Products (Demo)

## Bharat Term Secure Plus (PRD-TERM-001)

- **Type:** Pure term life insurance  
- **Currency:** All benefits and premiums in **INR**  
- **Objective:** Financial protection for dependents for a fixed policy term  
- **Eligibility (demo):** Entry ages 18–65 for standard underwriting; maximum sum assured subject to age and income proof  
- **Smokers:** Allowed; risk class may be **standard** or **substandard** based on disclosures  

### Definitions

- **Sum assured:** Death benefit payable in INR as selected at proposal stage.  
- **Nominee:** Person(s) entitled to receive policy proceeds as per applicable law and nomination recorded with the insurer.  

## India Life Sanchay Participating (PRD-TRAD-002)

- **Type:** Participating traditional savings-linked life insurance (synthetic)  
- **Suitability:** Long-term savings orientation with life cover; not a substitute for pure protection needs  
- **Underwriting:** Financial underwriting is emphasised for higher sum assured; incomplete documentation may lead to **postponement** (reason code **R-FIN-02** in demo data).  

## Flexi Growth ULIP (PRD-ULIP-003)

- **Type:** Unit-linked insurance plan (synthetic demo)  
- **Investment risk:** Borne by the policyholder; units are subject to market fluctuations.  
- **Restriction:** Current smokers are **not** eligible under simplified **banca** digital journey for this product in the demo manual — refer underwriting manual Section 2.  

## Regulatory posture (generic)

This document is for **internal training and agent demo** only. Product terms in production are governed by approved policy wordings and filings with the regulator.
